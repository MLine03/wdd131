Site Plan: Aloha Soda

Site Name

Name: Aloha Soda – Local Soda Shop

Description: Aloha Soda brings tropical vibes and sweet Polynesian energy through handcrafted sodas and treats. The name reflects the warm spirit of “aloha” and the fun, colorful experience of enjoying soda with community and joy.

Optional Domain Availability: alohasoda.com

Site Purpose

The website will celebrate Polynesian culture through vibrant soda flavors, inviting visuals, and community storytelling. It includes a menu, shop story, interactive flavor features, and a contact form.

Scenarios

  • What tropical soda flavors are available this week?
  • Where is Aloha Soda located and what are the shop hours?

Color Schema

The website uses bright, fruity colors to match the fun soda-shop vibe:

  • Strawberry Red (#e63946) – Headings, buttons
  • Cotton Candy Pink (#ffb6c1) – Backgrounds, highlights

Typography

  • Lobster – Used for site logo and headings
  • Open Sans – Used for all body text and navigation

Planned Pages & Features

  • Home: Welcoming message with tropical visuals.
  • Menu: Full list of sodas and snacks with descriptions.
  • About: History and cultural story of Aloha Soda.
  • Contact: Store info, map, and feedback form.
  • References: Citations for all media and external resources.

JavaScript Features

  • Rotating “Flavor of the Day”
  • Save favorite sodas in localStorage
  • Click-to-reveal promotional offers

Testing & Validation

  • W3C validation for HTML/CSS
  • Color contrast accessibility tests
  • Mobile-first responsive layout tests
  • Lighthouse audit for SEO & performance
