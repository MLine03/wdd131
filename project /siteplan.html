Aloha Soda

Site Name

Aloha Soda

The name "Aloha Soda" reflects the warm hospitality and joyful spirit of Polynesian culture, celebrated through a local soda shop offering tropical flavors and sweet treats.

Site Purpose

The site serves as an online presence for Aloha Soda, featuring a welcoming homepage, a menu showcasing unique Polynesian-inspired soda flavors and snacks, an about page sharing the story and spirit of the brand, a contact page with location and hours, and a references page.

Scenarios

  • What unique Polynesian soda flavors does Aloha Soda offer?
  • Where is Aloha Soda located, and what are the store hours?

Color Schema

  • Dark Red: Strong headings, important accents.
  • Medium Red: Main headings and borders.
  • Light Red: Highlights and backgrounds.
  • Bright Pink: Buttons and links.
  • Soft Pink: Subtle backgrounds and accents.

Typography

  • Meline Levao: Used for main headings and branding to reflect Polynesian style.
  • Roboto: Used for body text and general content for readability and modern look.

Wireframe

The homepage will feature a top navigation bar with the logo and site name, a hero section with a warm Aloha welcome and tropical images, a menu preview section, an about snippet, and footer with contact info.

Mobile view wireframe: vertical stacking with logo on top, then welcome message, menu highlights, and contact info below.

Desktop view wireframe: horizontal layout with navigation on top, hero banner center, menu section side by side with about snippet, and footer below.

CSS

External CSS will style the site with the red and pink color scheme, using the 'Meline Levao' and 'Roboto' fonts. Layout will be responsive with flexbox for desktop and stacked for mobile.

Testing

The website will be tested for accessibility, color contrast, SEO, responsiveness, and validated for HTML and CSS standards.
