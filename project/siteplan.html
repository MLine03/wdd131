Aloha Soda – Site Plan

Site Name

Aloha Soda

This name reflects the local soda shop's warm, welcoming spirit inspired by Polynesian culture and flavors.

Site Purpose

The site provides a vibrant, user-friendly hub showcasing Aloha Soda’s unique tropical soda flavors, snacks, store information, and dynamic features inspired by Polynesian traditions.

Scenarios

  • What unique soda flavors does Aloha Soda offer, including Polynesian-inspired options like Lilikoi Lagoon?
  • Where is Aloha Soda located and what are the store hours?

Color Schema

Primary Color: #D32F2F (Strong red) – used for headings and accents.

Secondary Color: #FFCDD2 (Soft red-pink) – used for backgrounds and highlights.

Accent Color: #B71C1C (Deep red) – used for links and buttons.

Typography

  • Roboto Slab – Used for headings (h1, h2, h3) to convey strength and warmth.
  • Roboto – Used for body text and paragraphs for readability and modern look.
