Unique Polynesian-Inspired Soda Flavors

  • Pineapple Passion – Sweet pineapple soda with a hint of passion fruit.
  • Coconut Breeze – Creamy coconut soda with a tropical twist.
  • Guava Glow – Refreshing guava-flavored soda, lightly sparkling.
  • Hibiscus Honey – Floral hibiscus soda sweetened with local honey.

  • Lilikoi Lagoon – Tart and sweet passion fruit (lilikoi) soda bursting with flavor.

Snacks Inspired by Polynesian Culture

  • Sweet Taro Chips – Crispy, lightly salted taro root chips.

  • Banana Lumpia – Fried banana spring rolls with honey drizzle.
