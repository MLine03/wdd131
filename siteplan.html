Aloha Soda – Website Planning Document

Site Name

Name: Aloha Soda

This name reflects a local soda shop inspired by Polynesian culture and hospitality. “Aloha” means love, peace, and compassion — the perfect brand identity for a vibrant soda experience rooted in community and flavor.

Optional Domain: alohasoda.com

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to introduce visitors to Aloha Soda — a Polynesian-inspired soda shop — and showcase its colorful menu, brand story, and community spirit. The site will include a homepage, menu, about page, contact form, and references. Interactive features like a rotating "Flavor of the Day" and favorite soda saving via JavaScript will create a fun, engaging experience.

Scenarios

  • What are the current flavors of the day at Aloha Soda?
  • How can I give feedback or find directions to the soda shop?

Color Schema

The color palette evokes warmth, friendliness, and a tropical vibe:

  • Red (#e63946): Used for headings, buttons, and important accents.
  • Light Pink (#fcd5ce): Used as background color for sections to give a soft, welcoming feel.
  • Teal (#1fbfb8): Used for links and icons to maintain a tropical freshness.

Typography

  • Lobster: Used for all main headings and logo-style text to add flair and uniqueness.
  • Open Sans: Used for paragraphs, navigation, and general content for clean readability.
