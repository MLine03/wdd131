About Aloha Soda

Hello! My name is Meline Levao, and I am the creator of Aloha Soda. As a Polynesian, I wanted to share the love, mana, and rich culture of my people through this soda shop. Aloha Soda is inspired by Polynesian hospitality, bringing the joy of tropical flavors and vibrant beverages to the community.

At Aloha Soda, we aim to create an experience that not only satisfies your thirst but also brings you a taste of paradise. Whether you're enjoying our Aloha Dew or our signature Lilikoi Fizz, every sip is a little piece of aloha spirit!
